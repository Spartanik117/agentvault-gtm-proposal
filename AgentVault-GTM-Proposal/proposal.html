1. Target User Segments

Segment 1: DeFi Yield Farmers & LPs

Target Profiles & Sub-Segments

Yield Arbitrageurs: LPs deploying assets across Curve, Uniswap V3/V4, and Balancer pools. They actively rebalance to capture fee spikes.
Momentum DEX Traders: Swing traders utilizing yield-bearing tokens (e.g. Pendle) requiring automated execution templates for stop-losses or dips.

Pain Point Solved by AgentVault

Yield optimization is gas-intensive and slow to execute manually. LPs suffer from impermanent loss, frontrunning (MEV), and yield slippage. AgentVault enables them to build 24/7 autonomous agents via natural language prompts to rebalance and hedge pools instantly.

Primary Channels (Online/Offline)
  • Online: Gated Discord server channels of Uniswap/Morpho; Warpcast (Farcaster) /defi hub; Crypto Twitter search trackers.
  • Offline: Hacker lounges and technical workshops at global conferences (ETHDenver, EthCC, Devcon) and Web3 dev groups.

Segment 2: University Blockchain Clubs

Target Profiles & Sub-Segments

DAO Governance Leads: Active student delegates managing and directing proposal voting power for partner DeFi protocols.
Aspiring Quant-dev Builders: Tech-forward students seeking practical experience developing trading algorithms without institutional overhead.

Pain Point Solved by AgentVault

Students face technical smart contract barriers and lack capital to deploy live trading bots. Lecture schedules prevent 24/7 market monitoring. AgentVault's no-code builder lets clubs collaboratively design, test, and run agents on testnets safely.

Primary Channels (Online/Offline)
  • Online: Combined Discord/Telegram hubs of elite groups (Blockchain at Berkeley, Stanford Blockchain, Michigan Blockchain, Franklin DAO).
  • Offline: Club general meetings, intercollegiate hackathons, regional university summits, and college campus workshops.
